# Day 14 Supplement: Live Scenario - Interview Accelerator

## 80/20 Summary

**The hardest interview section:**
- They show you a transaction pattern (3-5 min review)
- You spot red flags OUT LOUD
- They assess your thinking in real-time

**The key skill:**
> Think out loud. Use your framework. Reference what you're seeing.

**What they're grading:**
- Can you SPOT red flags?
- Can you ARTICULATE them clearly?
- Do you use LOGIC, not just guessing?
- Do you STAY CALM under time pressure?

---

## The Live Assessment Protocol

**Given: Customer profile + transaction history**
**Your response:**

1. **First pass (30 sec):** Establish baseline
   - What type of customer?
   - What's normal for them?

2. **Second pass (60 sec):** Spot flags
   - Velocity issues?
   - Recipient problems?
   - Documentation gaps?
   - Profile mismatches?

3. **Third pass (30 sec):** Synthesize
   - What's most concerning?
   - What type of activity?
   - What's next step?

---

## Interview Gold

**Q: "What red flags do you notice?"**
> Think out loud: "I see that in the last 30 days, there's been [X]. This is unusual because [matching to profile]. Then [Y] happened - that's [red flag]. The pattern suggests [type of activity]."

**Q: "What's happening here?"**
> Structured approach: "This looks like [LAYERING / PLACEMENT / ETC] because [specific evidence]. The key indicators are [list 2-3 specific things]."

**Q: "What would you do next?"**
> "My next steps would be: 1) [verify entity against registry], 2) [request documentation], 3) [contact customer for explanation], 4) [escalate if needed]. I'd escalate today because [severity reason]."

---

## What Interviewers Want to Hear

- **Structured thinking** (framework, not random)
- **Specific observations** (not "it looks weird")
- **Red flag language** (velocity, retention, pass-through, etc.)
- **Clear next steps** (concrete actions)
- **Calm under pressure** (not rushing, not silent)

**Key:** They want to see how you think, NOT to be right every time. They'll keep asking follow-ups.

---

## Quick Red Flag Cheat Sheet

**Quickly scan for:**

1. ✅ **Velocity** - Same-day in/out
2. ✅ **Retention** - Under 24 hours
3. ✅ **Recipients** - Many new/unrelated
4. ✅ **Source** - Unknown, can't verify
5. ✅ **Profile mismatch** - Activity vs declared purpose
6. ✅ **Documentation** - Missing or mismatched

**Start with:** "This customer's profile says [X]. Looking at activity, [Y] doesn't match because [Z]."

**End with:** "Most concerning is [TOP FLAG] - I'd [NEXT ACTION]."
