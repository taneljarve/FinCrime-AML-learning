# Day 12 Supplement: Behavioral Questions - Interview Accelerator

## 80/20 Summary

**What 80% of your interview impact comes from:**
1. **STAR method** = Situation → Task → Action → Result
2. **3-4 strong stories** ready to go
3. **Own YOUR contributions** (not "we")
4. **Connect to Wise values** in answers

**STAR format:**
- **S**ituation: Set the scene
- **T**ask: What was the challenge?
- **A**ction: What did YOU do specifically?
- **R**esult: What happened? Learnings?

## Interview Gold

**Q: "Tell me about a time you caught something important others missed."**
> STAR answer from attention to detail: Set scene, explain YOUR specific observation (not what team did), result + value to Wise.

**Q: "Describe a time you worked under pressure."**
> STAR answer from trading/sales: High-pressure situation, step-by-step what you did, outcome + learnings.

**Q: "Why Wise?"**
> Research BEFORE: Understand mission (global money transfer revolution), Know 4 values, Connect YOUR story to WHY this role fits.

**Bad generic answers:**
- "I just want to work in fintech"
- "Your company seems cool"
- "I need a job"

**Good answers:**
- Know the mission specifically
- Connect to personal experience
- Show genuine excitement

---

## What Interviewers Want to Hear

- **Ownership** (not "we did" - you specifically)
- **Specific examples** (not hypotheticals)
- **Connection to Wise values** (not generic)
- **Learning mindset** (show what you learned)

**Practice out loud 3x each story minimum.**
