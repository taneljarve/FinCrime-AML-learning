## If You Get Stuck

**The magic phrase:**
> "I'd approach it by [framework step]. Let me think through what I'd check..."

This shows PROCESS thinking even if you don't have the answer immediately.

**NOT:**
- "I don't know" (just stops there)
- "It depends" (no opinion)
- Wild guessing (makes stuff up)

**ALWAYS:**
- Show your thinking
- Reference framework
- Say "I'd investigate by..."

---

## Closing Strong

**At the end, ask good questions:**
- What's a typical day like for this team?
- What does success look like in the first 90 days?
- What's the biggest challenge on the team right now?

**This shows:**
- Genuine curiosity
- You're thinking about the role
- Not just hunting for a job
