# Day 13 Supplement: Wise Values - Interview Accelerator

## 80/20 Summary

**What 80% of your interview impact comes from:**
1. **Know ALL 4 values** - be able to explain each
2. **Connect to YOUR example** - real story, not theory
3. **Prioritize one** - which resonates MOST with you
4. **Sound authentic** - not scripted

| Value | Meaning | Your Story |
|-------|---------|----------|
| "Revolution" | Mission-driven, bigger than just a job | Financial inclusion |
| "Get it done" | Ownership, action | Taking initiative |
| "Customers > team > ego" | Customer-first, humble | Protecting customers |

## Interview Gold

**Q: "Which value resonates most with you?"**
> Pick ONE that you genuinely connect with. "Customers > team > ego" resonates most because I believe compliance protects customers, not just blocks transactions. In my experience, customer-first approach leads to better outcomes.

**Q: "How do you live 'No drama. Good karma' at work?"**
> Clear, direct communication. Assume good intent until proven otherwise. Don't gossip or create drama. If I have concerns, I talk directly. If someone disagrees, I discuss respectfully. Stay focused on solutions.

**Q: "How does your background connect to Wise values?"**
> Trading = pattern recognition, data-driven. Sales = communication, customer empathy. Both require staying calm under pressure and focusing on the right outcome - which aligns with getting it done and protecting customers.

## What Interviewers Want to Hear

- **Authentic connection** (not memorizing)
- **Your specific example** (not generic)
- **Real excitement** about the mission
- **Understanding** of Wise's culture

**Red flags:**
- Can't name all 4 values
- Generic answer without connection
- Sounds scripted or rehearsed
- "Just want a job"
